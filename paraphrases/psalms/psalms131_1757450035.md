## Psalms 131:2
David says: I make my heart quiet. I sit still like a small child with mom. I feel safe with God. 🤫🧒👩‍👦❤️
## Psalms 131:3
David tells Israel: Trust God now and always. 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦⏰🌟
